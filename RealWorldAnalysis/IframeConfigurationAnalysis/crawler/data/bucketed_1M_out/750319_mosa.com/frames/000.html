Understated
by design

Our large floor
tile series 

What matters most in a space is rarely what asks for attention. It is what carries weight and sets the stage. It is what quietly directs the space. Grounded in material and craft, Mosa floor tiles do just that. They show how subtle design can define a room without ever seeking the spotlight. Through quality you can trust, tactility you can feel, and originality revealed through precision.

> Watch the full version of the video here

About Mosa

For over 140 years, Mosa has produced tiles by using the purest raw materials and the innovativeness of Dutch Design. Our tiles adorn walls, floors, façades, and terraces in private, commercial, and public buildings, inside and outside, in more than fifty countries over four continents.

> Read more about Mosa

Crafting timeless
ceramic surfaces

Mosa tiles are crafted with supreme design and engineering precision. As a result, this makes them quick and easy to install. At the same time, each product is technically and conceptually perfected for creating surfaces with a luxurious, seamless appearance that invites the touch.

> Discover the full Mosa collection

Produced locally, applied globally

Architects and designers all over the world create characteristic buildings with our tiles. To give you an idea of the various ways our tiles can be used, we collect examples of international projects that have incorporated tiles from our series.

> Discover our references and case studies

Pattern Generator

Want to design unique, creative patterns with our tiles? The Mosa Pattern Generator online tool allows you to do just that. The generator has a wide selection of grids and patterns to inspire you, which you can easily adapt to your preferences.

> Go to the Mosa Pattern Generator

Colour sample request

There is no substitute for experiencing our tiles firsthand. If you are uncertain about the colour, thickness, or texture of a tile, you can request a selection of tile colour samples from us. This will allow you to ensure they meet your exact expectations.

> Request your colour samples

Cradle to Cradle Certified® Gold certification

Sustainability is our foremost priority. To achieve this, we adhere to the Cradle to Cradle philosophy, which helps us make a positive contribution to our society, our economy, and our planet. Mosa is the first ceramic tile company in the world to have gained Cradle to Cradle Certified® Gold certification for nearly its entire tile collection.

> Read more about Mosa’s sustainability efforts

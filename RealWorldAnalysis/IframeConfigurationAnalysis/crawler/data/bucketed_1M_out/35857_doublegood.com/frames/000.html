How it works

From setup to success in just 3 simple steps

1

Download the app and invite your team

Download the Double Good app on your phone, answer a few questions to set up your fundraiser, then send invites to your team so everyone is ready to go.

2

Launch and share your Pop-Up Store®

Each team member creates their own pop-up store to share via text, email, or social media, so friends and family can support from anywhere.

3

Watch as the funds start rolling in

Purchases ship directly to your supporters and you keep 50% of every bag sold. Track sales and follow the leaderboard in the app as you climb closer to your goal.

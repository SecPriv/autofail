      Euroffice UK | Office & Workplace Supplies for UK Businesses

      Euroffice UK is a leading online supplier of office supplies, workplace essentials, and catering products, helping businesses of all sizes keep their operations running smoothly. From everyday office stationery to specialist equipment, we make business purchasing simple, affordable, and reliable.

      With over 60,000 products available for next day delivery, our range includes office supplies, printer paper, ink and toner, office furniture, as well as kitchenware, tableware, and hospitality and catering supplies. We provide everything needed to support modern workplaces, breakrooms, and food service environments.

      We support businesses across the UK, including London, Manchester, Leeds, Newcastle and surrounding areas, with flexible payment options such as 30 day business credit terms. Customers can also benefit from exclusive savings through Euroffice Rewards and regular offers.

      Shop with confidence from trusted brands such as HP, Canon, Q Connect, Navigator, Post it, Bic, and Rexel, backed by over 13,000 Trustpilot reviews and an Excellent rating for service and reliability.

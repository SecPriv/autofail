Vision & Mission

Vision

In accordance with the expectations of the people of Pakistan and the vision of the Federal Government, comprehensively transform FPSC into organizationally responsive, technologically enabled and continuously evolving institution that accomplishes the assigned mandate by ensuring policy driven, merit based, timely and transparent recruitment and selection of suitable HR for the Federal Government and for the Government of Gilgit Baltistan

Mission

In accordance with the vision and laid down policies, ensure competitive, merit-based, transparent recruitment and selection of suitable HR for the Federal Government and for the Government of Gilgit Baltistan

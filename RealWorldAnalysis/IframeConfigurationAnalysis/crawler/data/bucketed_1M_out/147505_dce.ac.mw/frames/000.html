Domasi College of Education (DCE), funded by the Malawi Government, replaced the former Domasi Teacher Training College. Domasi College of Education is the largest Teachers\' Training College in Malawi, offering both Diploma and Degree levels of training in secondary education and Degree level training in Primary education.

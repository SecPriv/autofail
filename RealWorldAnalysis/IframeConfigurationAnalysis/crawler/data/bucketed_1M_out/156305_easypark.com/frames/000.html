Pay per use: EasyPark Small

15% service fee

With EasyPark Small's pay-per-use option, customers registered with EasyPark in Austria pay a service fee of 15% of the parking cost, with a minimum fee of 29-55 cents when paying for parking. This fee is added to the total cost of your parking session. Save time the easy way and download the app now!

For frequent users: EasyPark Go

€3,99/ month

With the EasyPark Go subscription, you pay a fixed subscription fee per month instead of a service fee per parking session. The service is exactly the same as when you pay per use, but for €3,99/month, you get an unlimited number of parking sessions with no additional service fee to EasyPark*.

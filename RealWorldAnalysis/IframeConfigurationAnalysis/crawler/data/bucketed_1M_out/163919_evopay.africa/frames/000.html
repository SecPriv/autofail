About Evopay

Evopay is a cutting-edge digital payment platform designed to revolutionize parking payments with speed, security, and convenience. Whether you’re in a bustling city center or a secure parking facility, Evopay ensures a hassle-free experience by enabling users to pay for parking effortlessly through digital and mobile payment solutions.

Driving smart urban mobility by providing a seamless, cashless, and efficient way to manage parking payments—anytime, anywhere.

Mobile App

Evopay Africa Mobile app enables you to pay for parking by Mpesa, Visa & Vouchers. View the available car parks as well a directory on the shops available per car park. You may also manage your prepaid parking cards with top up, balance and statements available.

Prepaid Cards

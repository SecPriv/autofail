About maternity and childbirth benefits!

The new social insurance system being introduced in Uzbekistan fully guarantees your labor rights and social benefits.

From now on, maternity benefits and temporary disability payments for women working in the private sector are covered by the Social Insurance Fund.

“Inson uchun” Online Exhibition

The traditional annual “Inson uchun” exhibition is now available in a digital format! This online platform brings together entrepreneurs who provide employment opportunities for people with disabilities and contribute to the development of society. From any region of the country, you can connect with socially responsible businesses, explore their products, and establish new partnerships.

Baraka – all your benefits now in one card!

Baraka is the first official card and app in Uzbekistan for receiving and managing social benefits. Government-provided social payments and assistance are transferred to the Baraka card. It also functions as both a bank card and a means of identity verification. You can manage your Baraka social card through the Baraka app.

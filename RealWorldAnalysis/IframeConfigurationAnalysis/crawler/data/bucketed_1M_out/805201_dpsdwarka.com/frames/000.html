It is a matter of immense pride that Mr V.K.Shunglu, Hon`ble Chairman, The Delhi Public School Society, has been conferred with the prestigious Padma Bhushan award.

Celebrating a Legacy of Excellence

DPS Dwarka stands as a symbol of distinction, steadfast in its mission to shape young minds and nurture bright futures. From nascence to becoming a thriving institution, our school remains committed to providing a vibrant learning environment where intellectual curiosity is ignited, creativity is nurtured and character-building takes centre stage. On the threshold of a glorious 30-year milestone, this year's theme, 'Thriving at Thirty' exemplifies nearly three transformative decades of diligence, resilience and excellence in education. It is a celebration of the enduring legacy we have created and the expansive horizons that await us.

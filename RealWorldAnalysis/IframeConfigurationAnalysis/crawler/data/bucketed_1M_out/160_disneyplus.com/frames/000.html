    Frequently Asked Questions

    What is Disney+?

    Disney+ is your go-to streaming destination of blockbuster movies, brand new originals and exclusive hit shows from Disney, Pixar, Star Wars, Marvel, Hulu, National Geographic and FX.

    What can I watch on Disney+?

    With Disney+, a lifetime of great stories awaits.

    • Blockbuster movies, brand new originals and exclusive hit shows

    • Timeless classics and recent hits from Walt Disney Animation Studios

    • An ever-growing collection of general entertainment from Hulu, including unscripted reality shows, true crime, stand-up comedy, horror, and so much more

    • Heart-warming storytelling for all ages from the masterminds at Pixar

    • The entire Star Wars Saga and much of the Marvel Cinematic Universe

    • Documentaries from National Geographic

    What is Hulu?

    Hulu sits alongside Disney, Pixar, Marvel, Nat Geo and Star Wars on Disney+. It is home to a large library of current and past TV shows, films and documentaries. Including Hulu Originals like All's Fair, Paradise, Good American Family, A Thousand Blows, and Alien: Earth. Genres include unscripted reality shows, true crime, stand-up comedy, horror, and so much more. All of which is included in your Disney+ subscription and can be found under the "Hulu" tile in your Disney+ app. Hulu content availability may vary by region.

    How much does Disney+ cost?

    You can choose from two Disney+ plans:

    • Premium (€15.99/month or €159.90/year): Up to 4K UHD & HDR video. 4 concurrent streams. Downloads on up to 10 devices. Up to Dolby Atmos audio. Ad-free movies and series.

    • Standard (€10.99/month or €109.90/year): Up to 1080p Full HD video. 2 concurrent streams. Downloads on up to 10 devices. Up to 5.1 audio. Ad-free movies and series.

    All plans may include: advertising both within live content (inc. reruns) and special events as well as before and after playback of content, theatrical trailers, promotion of Disney products and services, sponsorship and alike.     Is there any commitment when signing up for Disney+?

    There are no commitments and you can cancel at any time, effective at the end of your current payment period. Just follow the below 5 easy steps:

    1. Go to www.disneyplus.com and log in

    2. Select your Profile

    3. Select Account

    4. Select Cancel Subscription

    5. Select Complete Cancellation to confirm

How Pre-Qualification Works

Getting Pre-Qualified is quick and easy. There's no impact to your credit score and you can see your offer before you apply.

  1. Answer a few questions

    Provide some basic info like your birthday and annual income

  2. Find out if you Pre-Qualify

    Review the details, including the credit limit, rates, and fees.

  3. Complete your application

    To finish up, answer a few more questions and submit your application. Completing an application requires a hard inquiry, which could impact your credit score.

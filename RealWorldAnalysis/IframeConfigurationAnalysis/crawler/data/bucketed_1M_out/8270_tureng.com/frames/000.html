Tureng Multilingual Dictionary is a reliable online dictionary and translation platform designed to support everyday language needs as well as professional and academic terminology. It is widely used for general translation, word lookup, language learning, and precise terminology research. Students, academics, and professional translators rely on Tureng for its accuracy, consistency, and well-structured content.

Tureng provides accurate translations in English, Spanish, Turkish, French, and German. It includes millions of carefully organized and reviewed term translations, idioms, phrases, and domain-specific terminology across law, trade, medicine, engineering, finance, technology, and many other industries, making it suitable for both professional and academic use.

Powered by artificial intelligence (AI) and advanced machine translation technologies, Tureng helps users understand meaning, context, and real-world usage. Users can search terms by professional field, listen to multiple pronunciation accents in several languages, and contribute by adding or correcting entries to keep the content accurate and up to date.

Tureng is available on iPhone, Android, iPad, and macOS, and is trusted as a leading English-Turkish online dictionary and a comprehensive multilingual reference for accurate, professional, and AI-enhanced translation.

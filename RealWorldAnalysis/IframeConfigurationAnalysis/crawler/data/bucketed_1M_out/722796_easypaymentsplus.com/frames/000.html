About

 

When you visit our website, it may store or retrieve your personal data through our use of cookies and other tracking technologies, which we collectively refer to as “cookies”. They are widely used for a variety of purposes including to make websites work or to remember you from one visit to the next.

 

Cookies may be set by us, third parties and/or your browser. Cookies help make our website work, or make it work more efficiently, and they may recognise and remember your preferences from one visit to the next.

 

Some of these cookies are essential to make our website work correctly, so they cannot be turned off. Others are non-essential but are very useful to us and you can choose whether to enable these or not.

 

Select the cookie categories for more information on the different types of cookies we use. Our default setting is to have all non-essential cookies turned off unless you choose to enable them. You can either “Allow all cookies” or use the sliders to make your choice and click “Adjust my preferences”. You can reject all non-essential cookies by choosing to “Use necessary cookies only”. This will allow a cookie to remember what choices you made.

 

You can manage your preferences at any time by clicking the cookie link at the bottom of every page on the website.

 

To find out more about how we use cookies please see our cookie policy.

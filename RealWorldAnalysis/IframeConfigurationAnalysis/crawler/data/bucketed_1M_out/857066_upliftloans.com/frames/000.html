Online Installment Loans From a Lender

Uplift Loans offers no hassle, online installment loans between $200 and $1500 for first time borrowers. Returning customers can borrow up to $3500. All borrowers can qualify with good or bad credit history and have the opportunity to receive their money the same day!

With a valid checking account and contact information, your application is quickly processed, with money deposited directly into your account immediately with instant funding*, the same day on loans approved by 1:30 P.M. CT, or the next business day. Time frames can be affected by your financial institution.

Uploans

Uploans are designed to help customers like you receive additional funds up to your available credit limit. Plus, you don’t need to pay off your current loan with us to be eligible for an Uploan.

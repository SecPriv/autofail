Klaus Hammes

Visionary and pioneer in clean air

April 7, 1939 - April 30, 2026

With the passing of Klaus Hammes, IQAir has lost its visionary founder, who, from the 1960s through the 1990s, improved air quality for people around the world through the innovative use of air filter technology in buildings and vehicles. Klaus Hammes was a pioneer of the Indoor Air Quality (IAQ), Cleantech and HealthTech industries and continues to inspire the work of IQAir to this day.

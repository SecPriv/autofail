About

Mafia MMO is a free online text-based RPG where every choice shapes your reputation. Create your character, grow your stats, earn cash, and rise through the criminal underworld in a persistent city filled with rival players and opportunities.

Train in the gym to build strength, attack rivals, join or create gangs, hunt and roam the streets for loot, and test your luck in the casino. Run crimes, complete missions, trade items, and climb the ranks as you build your empire—whether you play solo or with a crew.

How to convert and download a YouTube video?

  1. Open YouTube (youtube.com) and find a video you would like to download. Copy the link of the YouTube video from your browser's address bar. Our converter supports the following YouTube links:
    • youtube.com/watch?v=videoId
    • youtube.com/embed/videoId
    • youtube.com/live/videoId
    • youtube.com/shorts/videoId
    • youtu.be/videoId
    Please note: Live videos are only supported if the live stream has already finished.
  2. Paste your link into our converter above. After that, choose your download format. You can choose between:
    • MP3 (240 minutes / 192 kbps)
    • MP4 (30 minutes / 360p)
    If you do not choose any format, the video will be converted to an MP3 file. Click the “Convert” button.
  3. Our converter will start the process. Depending on the length of the video, the conversion can take a while. As soon as the conversion is successfully finished, you'll be able to download the file by clicking the “Download” button.

Parent & Guardian Escalation Guide Now Available

How can parents and guardians effectively voice their questions or concerns?

Polk County Public Schools encourages families to start with the staff member closest to the situation. Often, that’s the quickest way to find a solution and ensure the right people are informed and involved. If the concern isn’t resolved, there’s a clear, step-by-step path to escalate the concern.

Click here for the Parent & Guardian Escalation Guide and learn how to make your voice heard on concerns or questions about classroom instruction, school procedures, student support, or district-level issues

 

PCPS provides access to many resources to support students as well as resources to support parents.

Learn about our 1:1 device program and discover digital learning resources such as ClassLink, Schoology, Office365, Parent Portal and much more. 

Our library media resources provide access to research materials including eBooks, videos, articles and more.

Library materials are available for review by parents and the community. Learn more about the district’s materials examination and challenge process and how to download and submit the Request for Examination of Materials form.

The library parent opt-out is available year round. To access the opt-out, go to Parent Portal.

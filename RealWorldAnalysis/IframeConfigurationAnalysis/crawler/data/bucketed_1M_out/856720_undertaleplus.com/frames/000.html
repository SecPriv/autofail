Play Undertale the way you want to!
Undertale Plus is a fan-made mod for Undertale that adds lots of settings, ranging from functional, aesthetic, quality-of-life, and many more! Examples include:
  • 60 FPS and Widescreen support
  • Visible Armor in the Overworld
  • All Console-Exclusive Content
  • Bug Fixes
  • Colored Sprites and Battle Backgrounds
  • Reimagined Soundtrack
All of which are optional, and can be toggled however you'd like!

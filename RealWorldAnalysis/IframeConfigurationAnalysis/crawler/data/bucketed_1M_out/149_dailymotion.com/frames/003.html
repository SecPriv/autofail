Data privacy at Dailymotion

To deliver our Service and give you a personal experience, we and our Partners may use trackers to:

  • store and/or access information on your device
  • process personal data like your IP address, location, or device info.

Our non-IAB Partners and 573 IAB Partners may use trackers and your personal data for reasons like:

  • personalised advertising and content
  • advertising and content measurement
  • audience research
  • service development.

They may use data collected by us or collect their own data separately. Some of them may also use special features like precise geolocation data and identification through device scanning.


Processing this data relies either on your consent or on legitimate interest. You can say no to processing that relies on legitimate interest here.


Essential trackers

We use essential trackers to deliver our Service features according to our Terms of Use. We don't need your consent to use this kind of tracker.


Processing your personal data

We rely on compelling legitimate interest or on legal obligation to process your personal data to ensure our Service's security.


We rely on legitimate interest to use your personal data to:

  • select basic ads
  • understand our audience and improve our service
  • measure content and ad performance (for your own content, we rely on the contract with you).

We rely on the contract to deliver our Service, including to:

  • create content profiles and recommend content
  • technically deliver content and ads
  • share content on social media.

We need your consent to process your personal data for any other reason, and to use non-essential trackers.


We may combine tracker data with other data we might have about you for any of our processing purposes. We may share this info, including your browsing data or email address, with our Selected Commercial Partners.


How to manage your privacy choices

Your choices apply anywhere you see this banner. You can change your choices or withdraw your consent at any time by clicking or tapping Manage Cookies. If you don’t consent, we still process your personal data for purposes that don’t rely on it, like letting you use our Service, showing you non-targeted ads, and recommending content.


Click or tap Accept to agree to this collection, sharing, and use of your personal data. To use Dailymotion without agreeing, click or tap Continue without accepting. If you want to fine-tune your privacy choices, click or tap Personalise.

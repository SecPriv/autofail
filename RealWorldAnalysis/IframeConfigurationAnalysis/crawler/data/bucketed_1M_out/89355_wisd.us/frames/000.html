  • Schools
    WISD is made up of 20 schools, 11 elementary campuses, four middle schools, and four high schools. All of our campuses are equipped with all the learning tools necessary for our students to have a rich and high-quality learning school year. With many extracurricular activities to choose from, our students get to experience a “complete” education.

    Greetings, all! Let us celebrate the accomplishments and accolades of Cruz Javier Garcia. His academic excellence began at Airport Elementary, where his teachers embedded within him the work ethic and skills needed to accomplish Herculean Feats. When asked about what the most important thing about his formative years were, he stated, “My teachers instilled the love of reading and learning early on. They helped me believe I could do anything as long as I stayed ambitious, determined, and did everything with integrity.”

    🏆 The Highlights:
    • Weslaco High School grad, District Wrestling Champion & MVP.
    • Earned his Bachelor’s degree at just 20 years old.
    • Taught at WISD.
    • Coached wrestling at WHS.
    • Taught and coached wrestling at Katy ISD.
    • Just graduated from LSU with his Master’s degree (4.0 GPA!).
    • Heading to Concordia St. Paul this fall to start his Ph.D.!

    Thank you to the amazing Weslaco teachers who built his foundation. Your next chapter is going to be incredible, Cruz! Geaux Tigers!!! 🐯🎓💪

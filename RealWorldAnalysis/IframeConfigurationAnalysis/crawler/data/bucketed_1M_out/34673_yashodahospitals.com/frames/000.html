Why Choose Yashoda Hospitals?

Yashoda Hospitals constantly works to provide patients with the best healthcare service by perfectly blending revolutionary technology, best medical expertise and advanced procedures. The needs of the patients have always guided the Group towards delivering comprehensive care. They provide apt medical diagnosis and treatment for diseases, trauma, and emergencies across practically every medical discipline.

Why is Yashoda Hospitals known as the best hospital in Hyderabad?

Yashoda Hospital is one among the Best Multispecialty Hospitals in Hyderabad, committed to offering inimitable and evidence based health care services. We are a leading and integrated healthcare center with multiple Centers of Excellence for super specialities including Heart, CT surgery, Neuroscience, Cancer, Liver, Multi-organ transplants, Bones & joints, Nephrology, Robotic sciences, Spine surgery, Mother & Child and Fertility.

With advanced facilities and treatments, our hospital is considered as one among the Best Hospitals in Hyderabad. Well-equipped with 2,756 Beds, Intensive care unit/operation theatre, mobile ultrasound, X-ray, 2D echo etc for critically ill patients, we have multispecialty hospitals located in Somajiguda, Secunderabad, Malakpet, and Hitec City. This provides accessibility to our 24/7 emergency care services that include blood bank, laboratory, diagnostics, and ventilator management.

With a multi-disciplinary team of experts, we have adopted latest advances in treatments, such as stereotactic radiosurgery, HIPEC, triple f radiosurgery, robotic surgery, VATS, capsule endoscopy, TAVR, TMVR, enterocytoscopy, endocytoscopy, retroperitoneoscopic surgery, arthroscopy, bone marrow transplant, heart transplant, kidney transplant, liver transplant, etc.

Yashoda Hospitals, Hyderabad provides for Comprehensive care, Clinical Excellence, Qualified and trained doctors and cutting-edge technology which is available to treat high-risk health conditions apart from managing pre-existing and chronic conditions, which makes us one of the Top Hospitals in Hyderabad. Our specialist team consisting of Cardiologists and Cardio Thoracic surgeons, Neurologists and Neuro Surgeons, Oncologists, Gastroenterologists, Dermatologists, Orthopedicians, Endocrinologists, Ophthalmologists, Nephrologists, Rheumatologists, ENT Surgeons, Liver transplant surgeons, Critical care specialists, Pulmonologists, Radiologists, Plastic surgeons, Urologist, and many more super speciality doctors who strive to provide the best and advanced treatments and procedures.

Look no further if you want the Best Hospital in Hyderabad which offers patient centric care, latest in advanced technologies and the complete spectrum of medical specialities.

When should you go to Hospital?

A person should rush or go to the nearest hospital when he has a medical emergency, for eg. difficulty in breathing, shortness of breath, pain or pressure in the chest or upper abdominal region, fainting, sudden dizziness or weakness. You can also visit a doctor or seek online consultations for medical issues that do not require immediate medical attention.

How to find best hospital in Hyderabad?

To find the best hospital in Hyderabad you should first determine the care you need and the hospitals present around you. Think about your personal and financial constraints if any. Discuss your hospital options, and choose a hospital that fits your criteria wholistically.

What is multi super speciality hospital?

A Multi-speciality Hospital has facilities for most conditions and diseases to be taken care of by an expert team of doctors and specialists. The patients are entirely taken care of in air-conditioned rooms and canteens inside with personalized care.

Why Yashoda Hospitals Called the Best Multispeciality Hospitals in Hyderabad?

Yashoda Hospitals is known as the best multispeciality hospital since healthcare for the people in their diverse medical needs. Their comprehensive health care team of specialized doctors, round-the-clock nursing staff and paramedics are equipped with the latest medical innovations and state-of-the-art infrastructure. Yashoda Hospitals has an excellent Transplant Program for Heart, Lung, Liver and Kidneys and has contributed to a large number of life saving surgeries in the last decade.

Which is the best multi speciality hospitals in Hyderabad?

Yashoda Hospitals is one of the most well known Hospitals Groups in Hyderabad. They provide quality healthcare for people with diverse medical needs. They have four independent hospitals, 2,756 beds, four Cancer Institutes, four Heart Institutes, and more than 646 specialist doctors. It is a NABH Nursing Excellence Certified Hospital.

São Lucas Maternity Hospital

Integrated into the São Lucas Health Center, it is a space dedicated to providing humane care for pregnant women and newborns.
The maternity ward has modern and well-equipped facilities, including a modernized delivery room, dilation room, nursery, clinical support areas, and a green space designed to provide comfort and well-being to patients.

The Catholic University of Mozambique (UCM) was officially founded in 1995 as a private higher education institution (see Decree No. 43/95 of September 14). It is an institution of the Episcopal Conference of Mozambique (CEM) with its headquarters in the city of Beira, Sofala province.

In 2020, we created Supreme Values to be a value list based on market demand and the offers that items were getting to combat the growing levels of sharking in the MM2 trading community.

We quickly became proponents of accurate and accessible values and trading information, overcoming the pushback of those who wished to take advantage of those with little trading experience.

Today, Supreme Values proudly serves millions of MM2 players around the world!

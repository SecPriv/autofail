Check PECTAA 8th Class Result 2026 Online

This year, the exams of all 8th class students have been conducted by PECTAA and now the Punjab Boards are going to announce the results. Students will be checking their result online for the first time, so some students are worried about how they will check their result.

Checking the result online is new for students and they have no idea how the result is checked. All these students should know that checking the result online is very easy and here we have provided you with a complete guide which you can follow to check your result easily.

In this modern time, the internet, smartphones, and laptops are available to almost every student, so now you can check your result easily from home without any worry. If for any reason you do not have internet access, you can still check your result from home.

Below we have explained all the methods of result checking through which you can check your detailed result online from home easily.

There are four methods given by the Punjab Board to check the result online.

  1. Check the result by Roll Number
  2. Check the result through SMS
  3. Check the result by Name
  4. Check the result via the Gazette

Check 8th Class Result by Roll Number

Checking the result through roll number is a very easy method and through this method, you can see your detailed subject wise marks.

All students are given a roll number slip before the exam on which their unique roll number is written. In the search bar given above you have to type that same roll number, select your board, and click on the search button. As soon as you do this, your detailed 8th class result will appear in front of you, which you can also download in PDF format.

Check 8th Class Result by SMS

Very few people check their results through SMS. The main reason is that SMS charges are deducted and you are only informed about your obtained marks. If, for any reason you do not have internet access, then check your board number from the list given below, create an SMS on that number, type your roll number in the body, and send it.

Within a few minutes, you will receive an SMS in which your name and your obtained marks will be mentioned.

Check 8th Class Result By Name

Some students do not remember their roll number and due to the roll number being misplaced, they become worried about how they will check their 8th class result. There is no need to worry because now you can also check your result by your name.

In the search bar given below, type your exact name without any spelling mistakes and select your board.

As soon as you do this, a list of all students who were part of the 8th class 2026 examination in your board with your name. From this list you will have to select your school wise name, which may take some time, but you will get your result through your name.

Check the result via the Gazette

Checking the results through the gazette is a very difficult method, now used by very few people, but its biggest advantage is that you can find the results of all students from all boards in the gazette. You can also check the results of your relatives and friends through the gazette easily.

On 9th April, as soon as the result is announced, you will be able to download the gazette from here in which the results of all students of your board will be available, which you can check easily.

FAQ’s

When will the Punjab Board 8th Class Result 2026 be announced?

The 8th Class Result 2026 will be officially announced by the Punjab Boards and PECTAA on 9th April 2026 at 4:00 PM.

What are the total marks for the 8th Class Board Exams 2026?

The total marks for the 8th Class exams are 400. The exams were conducted for four subjects: Science, Math, English, and Urdu, with each subject carrying 100 marks.

What is the passing percentage required to pass the 8th Class exams?

To pass the 8th Class board exams, students must obtain at least 40% marks in each subject.

Which subjects were included in the 8th Class Board exams this year?

This year, the Punjab Examination Commission (PECTAA) conducted exams for four mandatory subjects.
1- Science
2- Math
3- English
3- Urdu

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I buy my bus ticket from obilet?
You can use our website or mobile applications to buy bus tickets from obilet. After entering the desired destination and date, you can compare all bus trips and buy your ticket by entering your passenger and card information.
Is obilet reliable?
You can buy your ticket safely on obilet. The purchases you make on our site are secured with an SSL certificate. In addition, 3D Security assurance is offered at the payment step when purchasing a bus ticket.
Is commission paid when buying bus tickets from obilet?
obilet does not charge commission. When buying a bus ticket using obilet, you only pay the price of your ticket.
Can I cancel my bus ticket?
You can cancel your bus ticket in accordance with the cancellation conditions determined by the companies. Cancellation conditions are presented to you at the payment page.
When will I be refunded when I cancel my bus ticket?
After your cancellation is confirmed, the refund will be transferred to your bank without any deductions. The paid amount will be reflected in your account within 7 working days, depending on your bank's return procedures. In transactions made with debit cards, this process may take 1-20 business days. The return process is completely dependent on your bank and obilet has no right to intervene.

Predictor

Where Superbru began. Pick the score in our famous predictor game. Over 50 tournaments covered every year across 7 sports: rugby union, football, cricket, rugby league, American football, Aussie Rules and rugby sevens.

Fantasy

Select a squad of real world players, then pick a starting team for each round. Manage your squad carefully with limited transfers and team limits. Major football and rugby union tournaments covered.

Daily Fantasy

Pick a handful of players in key positions each matchday. No squads, transfers or team limitations to worry about. Major team ball sports covered as well as golf, tennis, cycling and motorsport. Includes innovative odds-based format for some sports.

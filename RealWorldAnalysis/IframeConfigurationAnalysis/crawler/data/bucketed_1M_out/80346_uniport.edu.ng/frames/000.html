Tuition Fees at University of Port Harcourt

At the University of Port Harcourt we are committed to providing a high-quality education that is accessible to a diverse range of students.

See Details
Undergraduate Students
All Freshers
  • Full-Time Tuition (first year): ₦190,000 - ₦241,000
  • Part-Time Tuition (first year): ₦122,000 - ₦137,000
Returning Students
  • Full-Time Tuition (per session): ₦78,000
  • Part-Time Tuition (per session): ₦90,000
Graduate Students
Graduate School/Department
  • Full-Time Tuition (per session): ₦241,000
  • Part-Time Tuition (per session): ₦141,000
Additional Fees
  • Technology Fee: ₦49,000 per session
  • Student Activity Fee: ₦19,000 per session

Scholarships and Financial Aid

Each year, University of Port Harcourt is proud to honor academically talented and exceptionally skilled students with a variety of scholarships and awards. The university annually awards more than 1,000 scholarships to both undergraduate and postgraduate students.

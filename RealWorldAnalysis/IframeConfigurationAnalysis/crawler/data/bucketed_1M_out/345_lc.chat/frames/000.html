Live chat is a simple and powerful way to connect with your customers, whether it’s on your website or through a dedicated chat page. With an easy-to-use live chat platform, you can deliver smooth, instant live chat customer service that improves experiences and strengthens relationships.

When you interact with customers outside your website — whether through newsletters or social media posts — make sure it’s not a one-way relationship. Share a direct link to your chat and make yourself accessible.

This link gives customers instant access to live chat support — whether they have questions, need help, or just want to learn more about your business. Confident, well-informed customers are happier customers, and live chat customer support makes that possible, one chat at a time.

LiveChat includes a help desk feature to streamline customer support. While LiveChat focuses on real-time communication through its live chat software, it also integrates seamlessly with tools like ticketing systems to handle customer inquiries that require follow-up.

Using LiveChat’s help desk capabilities, you can:

  • Convert missed chats into tickets for later resolution.
  • Manage customer inquiries efficiently with a centralized ticketing system.
  • Combine live chat support services with email and other communication channels for a comprehensive support solution.

This integration ensures that no customer query is overlooked, providing a perfect balance between instant live chat for business and detailed follow-up support through the help desk system.

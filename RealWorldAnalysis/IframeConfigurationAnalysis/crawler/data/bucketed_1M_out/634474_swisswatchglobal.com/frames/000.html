About Swiss Watch

Two Decades of Passion & Dynamism

Swiss Watch is one of the world’s leading luxury watch retailers with nine Swiss Watch boutiques and more than 20 specialist boutiques across Malaysia, Singapore, Australia, and Thailand. Swiss Watch represents some of the world’s most loved luxury watch brands including Rolex, Tudor, Cartier, Bulgari, Omega, to name a few, and has amassed a sizeable portfolio of over 40 brands. ​

Swiss Watch offers an unparalleled collection of men’s luxury watch brands, showcasing the finest wristwatches for men luxury enthusiasts could desire. Swiss Watch is the ideal destination whether you’re searching for upscale men’s watches to elevate your wardrobe or the coolest men’s luxury watches to add to your collection. For those seeking nice watch brands for men, Swiss Watch curates a selection that blends craftsmanship with prestige. Additionally, Swiss Watch presents a stunning range of high-end watches for women, representing iconic women’s luxury watch brands that define elegance and timeless beauty.

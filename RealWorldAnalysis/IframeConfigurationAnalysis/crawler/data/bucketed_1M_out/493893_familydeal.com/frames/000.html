Shop Vehicles With LaFontaine Automotive Group

We're so excited that you're considering one of our many locations for your next vehicle purchase! You'll be amazed by how diverse our selection is across our locations. Each one of our dealers specializes in something different. We have Chevy dealers, Ram dealers, Ford dealers, Jeep dealers, and more!

Whichever manufacturer you're after, there's likely a LaFontaine location that carries it and has brand experts ready to assist you. We've made a point to develop a dynamic and competitively priced inventory across our locations. So if you're unable to find the model you want at the specific LaFontaine dealer you visit, our teams will simply call our other lots to track it down for you.

When you work with a large automotive group like ours, you get the robust support that comes with it, but always enjoy that personal touch that our sales specialists provide.

Discover New and Used Vehicles From Top Names

Whichever body style you're after, you can find it at one of our dealerships. We carry so many models from top names, and can help you find yours. We have sedans for those who are after commuter-friendly, efficient vehicles that are easy to park and great for busy city streets. We often have the most popular models like the Chevy Malibu and the Cadillac CT5.

Looking for a large SUV for family life? Consider classics like a Ford Expedition or Chevy Suburban. Need something smaller? Consider a Chevy Equinox, Ford Escape, or Jeep Bronco Sport!

Want to treat yourself to a thrilling ride? Browse our performance models like the Dodge Charger, Chevy Corvette, or Ford Mustang . If it's a truck you're after for work or fun, you can find the top-sellers across our locations, like the Ram 1500, Chevy Silverado, Jeep Gladiator, GMC Sierra 1500, and others.

We carry new and pre-owned vehicles, because we want shoppers of every budget size to find what they're looking for. Check out the latest models for up-to-date technology, like massive touchscreens, wireless smartphone connectivity, and voice-activated commands. 

Explore pre-owned models for tremendous savings. We focus on brands known for longevity, so our used inventory has models ready to go for many more miles.

Find Flexible Financing and Expert Service Here, Too

Our support doesn't end once you've found your model. From there, you can meet with our finance team to land a solid lease or loan. We have auto loan professionals at every dealership, ready to put together a payment package that supports your long-term goals. Plus, we take trade-ins, so you can easily turn your old car into the down payment for your next one!

When it's time for auto service, we have excellent maintenance departments across our locations. Our technicians are experts in the brands we carry, so they'll put you on the ideal service schedule. From oil changes to tire rotations and brake pad replacements, we handle all the tune-ups that keep your model moving forward.

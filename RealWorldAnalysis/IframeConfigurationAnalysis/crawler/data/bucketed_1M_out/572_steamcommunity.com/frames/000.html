Projekt: Passion - Season 2 Review

Space Traveler
Continue the story of this comical, adventurous, intimate, and amazing-looking visual novel about our protagonist and his friends (with benefits) as they continue their journey through space.
Projekt Passion is filled with hilarious moments & comedy, lots more intimacy, and a continued eye-locking, mind-blowing, ass-sitting experience that will not make you stop and will make you feel like 5 minutes more.

Story - Content
The story begins with our protagonist and Elysra sitting in each captain's chair while having a chat on their way back to Tartarus due to an emergency.

At the end of season one, a fierce battle was going on, and Ashe got knocked out. Leading to said emergency at the start of season two.
Ashe is taken to the same doctor who she owes money/hit jobs as a hitman, which she needs to pay off for him to help with her never-ending migraines.

But with the trip to Tartarus causing the fuel to be low and the credit card being in the hands of Kaylee, who is stuck on a desert planet with rebels. Our protagonist must face someone who wouldn’t be so happy about their encounter... Kaylee’s father.
End up his Kaylee’s father accepting to lend him some money IF they went to “buy” a new energy core for Kaylee’s family’s butler, FrAnS. Which the old one our protagonist stole in season one...

After the “purchase” of a new energy core and Ashe's awakening followed by fueling up the ship, the protagonist and his companions make their way back to Kaylee and the rebels.
On the way back, the power shuts off, and a massive space squid appears in front of the ship, leaving Emily traumatized and the rest of the group confused.

On their return to pick up Kaylee, the hideout is ruined, and almost all of the rebels except for the protagonist's friends are still alive and unhurt. Burning the brave soldier's bodies and making their way to Mytellor.


That would summon up briefly part 1 and maybe part 2 out of 6 parts. To experience the details of the story, the comedy, the lust, and, simply put, the actions, you’d have to play for yourself.
And I do recommend playing this whether you have season one or not; it’s worth playing.

You will encounter so many more intimate scenes, hundreds and thousands of hysterical hilarious jokes and moments, and overall just goofy fun.

Visual & Audio
Visuals
Short Videos
Projekt Passion is wholly different than other visual novels. The unusual thing about Projekt Passion is their short, voice-covered video animations, which are truly amazing-looking. Hence, why it is unfortunate that we only get a few in season two.

Characters & Animations
When it comes to the characters, I truly love them. The design and bodily & facial expressions look really good, especially the funny moments to increase the comedy of it.

And of course, the animations during certain spicy, hot, & moist scenes. Both the character build and its physics look incredible during these sorts of scenes, and it moves so cleanly yet with the detailed physics.
To be brief, it's a hilarious game with stunning characters and smooth sailing when two become one.

Textures & Surroundings
Where should I begin? The textures of everything make it all look realistic to their universal look. The details in structures, rooms, streets, just object detail and textural detail along with surrounding detail.
I find it all looking very good, and such good work with it.

Audio
Music & Voice Covers
The background music is so different throughout the game. And it’s not only instrumental music; no, but also singing in some of the songs. But the music can differ from instruments, singing & instruments, and surrounding noises.

Though in all the variety of music that is played sounds wonderful, well-made, and well spread out & mixed.

Sounds
This section is mainly for the incredible sound work the developers did for the intimate sounds that occur during intimacy. The two bodies hitting against each other, the soft loud noises from the protagonist's partner, the moving fluids.

Not to get too detailed (which I might have already done), the sounds are well made and sound incredible.

Pros and Cons
Pros-
  • Hilarious Story
  • Great Animations
  • Beautiful Textures & Visuals Overall
  • Amazing Character Models & Facial Expressions
  • Love The Short Animated, Voice Covered Videos
  • Beautiful Background Music

Cons-
  • Too Cheap (Deserves to Cost More)

My Final Little Thoughts
After playing the game for a few hours and digging deeper into the story and the fun, I really hate myself for not being the one who accepted to write the first game.

The time playing was a blast, and the number of comedy moments... At some points I could not breathe because of laughter. Hence is why I feel like the game deserves to cost SO MUCH more.
I’ve seen games that are worse but more expensive; I highly recommend trying out Projekt Passion: Season 2.

Rating: 10/10

Such Shame I Wasn’t The First One To Review Season One.

How do I open a trading account?

Yes, opening a trading account is easy. Register with your details, verify your identity, and start trading immediately!

What platforms do you offer for trading?

We offer our clients the award-winning trading platforms MT4 and MT5, which feature advanced tools and customizable options for an enhanced trading experience.

How fast are withdrawals processed?

MH Markets strives to process withdrawal requests promptly, typically within one business day. If we receive your request before 16:00 (GMT+2), it will be processed on the same day. Requests submitted after this time will be handled on the next business day. However, please note that the actual time for funds to reach your account may vary depending on your bank or payment method.
International Bank Wire Transfers (available for selected regions): Typically take 3–5 working days
Other Payment Methods: Generally received within 1 working day after processing.

What are the available payment methods for deposits and withdrawals?

At MH Markets, we aim to make funding your trading account as seamless as possible. We offer a variety of deposit and withdrawal options to suit your needs, including bank/wire transfers, credit cards, local banking, and QR payments. Please note that the availability of payment methods may vary depending on your region.
For detailed information about deposit and withdrawal options, simply visit your Client Area.

What is the minimum deposit required to start trading?

To begin trading with MH Markets, the minimum deposit requirements for each account type are as follows: for a Standard Account, the minimum deposit is USD 50; for an ECN Account, it is USD 1,000; and for a Prime Account, it is USD 100. Choose the account type that best suits your trading needs!

Does MH Markets endorse any AI tool for trading?

No, MH Markets does not endorse or recommend any AI trading tools at the moment.

How secure are my funds and personal information?

At MH Markets, we prioritize your privacy by collecting only essential personal information for providing our services, conducting identity checks, and preventing fraud. We uses cutting-edge encryption technology to protect all personal and financial data during transactions. This ensures robust protection against cyber threats and unauthorized access.

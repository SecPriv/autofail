Caring for Australians Since 2013

Partnered Health Medical Centres is one of Australia’s leading primary healthcare providers, with 57 clinics nationwide  including general practices and MoleScan skin cancer clinics.

With a long history in providing healthcare services, our team of dedicated doctors, nurses and staff are genuinely passionate about the health and wellbeing of our patients. Our services span all aspects of primary healthcare from general health assessments, paediatrics, travel medicine, skin checks, and chronic disease health assessments and management including diabetes and asthma.

Profile photo framework

Include 1 clear headshot, 1 full-body, 1 hobby/action shot, and 1 social photo with friends. Natural light wins.

Messaging momentum

Match → exchange 3–5 meaningful messages → propose a low-pressure plan. Keep the window moving.

First date ideas

Daytime coffee walk, bookstore browse + tea, gallery hour, or mini-golf. Built-in conversation beats sitting still.

Deal-breakers vs. preferences

List three true non‑negotiables and keep the rest flexible. It opens doors to real compatibility.

Safety essentials

  • Meet in a public place and tell a friend where you’re going.
  • Use in‑app calling until you’re comfortable sharing your number.
  • Avoid sending money or sharing sensitive info.
  • Trust your instincts—end the date or report if something feels off.

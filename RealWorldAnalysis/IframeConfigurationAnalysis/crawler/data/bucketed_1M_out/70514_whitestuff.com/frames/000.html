A LITTLE BIT ABOUT WHITE STUFF

We make distinctive clothes, designed in house. Obsessing over quality, the fabrics, great fit, and those unique details that matter. Because life would be dull if we all looked the same.

We've got women's clothing, men's clothing, footwear and accessories for summer. Think easy layers for unpredictable days, and warm-weather dressing sorted well in advance.

A denim dress is one of the easiest things you'll put on all summer. Throw one on with sandals and sunglasses and you're done. Our denim dresses come in different washes and lengths, with relaxed midi styles to something a little more dressed up. We've got denim skirts too, if that's more your thing.

When the sun shows up, our swimwear and beach collection is ready and waiting. Stylish swimsuits with clever shaping and nifty details. Bikini sets and tankinis that actually stay put. And floaty beach cover ups to throw on when you're done with the sun lounger.

Linen is such a summery fabric. It's cool, easy, and soft enough to wear all day. We’ve got linen trousers in wide-leg and tapered styles, breezy linen tops, and effortless linen tunics. And plenty of lovely linen dresses, of course, in all kinds of colours, prints and styles. For smarter occasions, a lightweight linen jacket is the perfect finishing touch.

Our tunics for women are a summertime staple. Longer than your average tee, they're endlessly versatile. We love to style them with jeans or linen trousers for an easy, put-together look. Speaking of which: a great pair of jeans is a must-have all year round. We've got all the silhouettes you’re after, from classic cropped jeans and barrel jeans to stretchy jeggings for when you’re chilling on a Sunday morning.

Dresses are our bread and butter (and our jam too). Petite dresses, midi dresses and maxi dresses. There's something for everyone and every occasion. If you're after a skirt and cute top, our maxi skirt styles are brilliant for holidays and evenings out.

At the heart of White Stuff, we're about trying to do the right thing by the planet and its people. We use certified materials and natural fibres as much as possible.

In our White Stuff online shop, you can browse the latest collections, find a bit of inspiration, and discover interesting things to read. And of course, there's always the White Stuff sale. We hope you find something you love.

Cookies

When you visit any website, it may store or retrieve information on your browser, mostly in the form of cookies. This information might be about you, your preferences or your device and is mostly used to make the site work as you expect it to. The information does not usually directly identify you, but it can give you a more personalized web experience. Because we respect your right to privacy, you can choose not to allow some types of cookies. Click on the different category headings to find out more and change our default settings. However, blocking some types of cookies may impact your experience of the website and the services we are able to offer.

You can set your browser to block or alert you about these cookies, but some parts of the site will not then work. These cookies do not store any personally identifiable information.

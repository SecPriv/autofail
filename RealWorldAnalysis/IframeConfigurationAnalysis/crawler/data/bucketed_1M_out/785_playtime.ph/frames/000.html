PlayTime Entertainment reflects the evolution of PlayTime from a single business into a broader entertainment platform.

Today, the company operates multiple business verticals, including PT Sports and PT Idols, all working together as one ecosystem.

PlayTime elevates entertainment for Filipinos through technology and innovation. We create unforgettable experiences across every genre as a leading promoter of entertainment and a true community destination where happiness takes center stage.

PlayTime empowers excellence on the global stage while uplifting local communities through PlayTime CARES, the company's corporate social responsibility arm.

CORPORATE SOCIAL RESPONSIBILITY

PlayTime CARES

PlayTime Cares

PlayTime CARES (Community, Action, Relief, Empowerment, and Sustainability) is the corporate social responsibility and social impact arm of PlayTime Entertainment, dedicated to translating the company's success in entertainment into meaningful, long-lasting contributions to Filipino communities.

ACTION

Wilderness Search and Rescue Philippines Inc. (WISAR PH)

Partnered with WISAR PH to support disaster response and rescue operations, helping save lives and protect communities in times of need.

COMMUNITY

Supporting Indigenous Livelihood in Pampanga

Together with Alagang Kapatid Foundation to bring farming supplies to the Aeta community in Floridablanca, Pampanga. Uplifting local communities by providing tools and resources that support sustainable livelihood for indigenous families.

COMMUNITY

Gawad Kalinga Playground

Partnered with Gawad Kalinga to build a playground for children, creating a safe and joyful space where young hearts can play, dream, and grow.

RELIEF

Alagang Kapatid Foundation

Through Alagang Kapatid Foundation, PlayTime CARES supported community welfare programs, providing aid and resources to families in need.

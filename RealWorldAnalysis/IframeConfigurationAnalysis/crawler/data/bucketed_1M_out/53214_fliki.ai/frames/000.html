Frequently asked questions

What is an AI video generator?

An AI video generator is a tool that turns text prompts, scripts, or existing content into finished videos - automatically generating the voiceover, visuals, music, and subtitles. Instead of filming, editing, and narrating manually, you describe what you want and the AI assembles the video in minutes. Fliki is an AI video generator used by over 12 million creators, professionals, and teams to make TikToks, YouTube videos, explainers, and training content.

How do I turn text into a video?

Paste any text - a blog post, a script, or a one-line prompt - into a text-to-video tool and it generates a matching video with AI voiceover, visuals, music, and burned-in captions. The whole process takes about two minutes for a short video, with no editing or design skills needed. Try it free with Fliki.

How do I add an AI voice to a video for free?

Upload your video to an AI voiceover tool, choose a voice, paste your script, and the tool generates a synced voiceover you can drop onto the video. Fliki lets you do this free with 2,000+ AI voices in 80+ languages, and even clone your own voice from a short recording for unlimited use.

How do I convert a blog post into a video?

Paste the blog URL or the full article text into a blog-to-video tool and it writes the script, picks matching visuals, narrates it with an AI voice, and adds subtitles - turning a 1,500-word post into a 60-second video. Fliki supports this in one click across 80+ languages, which is why it's one of the most popular ways creators repurpose long-form content.

How do I turn a PowerPoint into a video?

Upload your PPT or Google Slides file to a PPT-to-video tool and it converts each slide into a video scene with AI narration, transitions, and captions. Fliki handles this automatically - useful for training, onboarding, and online courses where you need the deck narrated without re-recording it yourself.

Can I clone my voice for videos?

Yes. Voice cloning tools record a short sample of your voice - usually 30 seconds to a few minutes - and generate an AI version you can use across videos, even in languages you don't speak. Fliki's voice cloning works in 70+ languages and stays consistent across every video you make with it.

How many languages do AI voice generators support?

The best AI voice generators support 70–90 languages, with multiple dialects and accents per language. Fliki supports 80+ languages and dialects including English, Spanish, Portuguese, Hindi, Arabic, Indonesian, French, German, Japanese, Korean, Russian, and Mandarin - with 2,000+ lifelike voices to choose from.

How do I make faceless YouTube videos and TikToks?

Faceless videos are made by combining AI voiceover, stock or AI-generated visuals, and auto-captions - no camera or on-screen presence required. Start with a script or prompt, let an AI video generator like Fliki produce the voiceover and visuals, and publish directly to TikTok, YouTube Shorts, or Reels. Most creators go from idea to upload in under five minutes.

Can AI-generated videos be used commercially?

Yes - as long as your tool's paid plan grants commercial rights and the music and stock media are properly licensed. Fliki's paid plans include full commercial rights and YouTube-licensed music, so you can publish to monetized channels, run ads, and do client work without copyright risk.

How long does it take to make an AI video?

Most AI video generators can produce a 30-60 seconds short video in under two minutes. Longer videos with multiple scenes usually render in five to ten minutes. With Fliki, the average short-form video is ready in about two minutes from the moment you hit generate.

All About Certini

Certini Bicycle Company was established from the sheer love of all things cycling, way back in 2001. Conceived by 2 life long friends and with over 25 years of cycle trade experience, their goal was simply to share their huge passion and infectious enthusiasm for this sport directly to you. This appetite for all things cycling was passed on to the next generation who work with them to continue to make Certini what it is today.

Fast forward over 20 years, and Certini is now 5 stores and 4 locations strong. Based in Plymouth, Bristol, Birmingham and now Stafford, operating our own Certini Stores and the Specialized Concept Stores of the South West and the Midlands. We now have a strong family based business which is still offering today that very passion and enthusiasm in getting you the very best products that the cycle industry has to offer.

Recent news

The council of ministers reached an agreement on 6 March during the 3rd reading of the pension bill.

In this agreement, the pension reform was definitively discussed and developed. 
The next step will now be the parliamentary debate. 

Once the bill has been voted, the current estimate of the earliest retirement date and the pension amount shown on mypension.be will no longer be correct for many.  
Because of this, we will, as soon as the pension reform has been voted, temporarily remove the following estimates for those potentially impacted by the reform:

  • the estimate of the earliest retirement date, 
  • the estimate of the pension amount, both at the earliest date and at the statutory retirement date.  

You can retire before 2027?
In that case, everything will remain visible because the reform does not influence your earliest retirement date and you will not be penalised. 
Do note that if you postpone your pension until 2027 or later, your pension amount can still change due to other reform measures.  
Find out more at www.sfpd.fgov.be/en/changes/pension-reform.

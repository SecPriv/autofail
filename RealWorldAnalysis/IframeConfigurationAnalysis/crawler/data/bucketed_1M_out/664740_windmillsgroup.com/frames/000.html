Windmills Group


Founded in 2016 by Bilal Moti MRICS, Windmills is a leading property and asset valuation and building surveying services firm in the UAE. Over the past decade, we have completed tens of thousands of valuations across all property types, serving more than 1,000+ clients.

Windmills is empanelled with nearly all major banks across the UAE. Along with banks, our client base includes financial institutions, developers, family groups, owners associations, auditing firms, insurance companies and more. All valuations are prepared in accordance with International Valuation Standards (IVS) promoted by RICS, together with applicable local valuation standards. Our reports provide full transparency, clearly setting out the valuation approach, methodology, assumptions and calculations. Moreover, all our reports are DLD-approved, Ajman RERA-approved and DARI-approved.

Our Chairman and CEO, Mr. Bilal Moti MRICS, brings 25 years of international banking expertise and over 15 years of leading real estate valuation and building surveying operations across the UAE and GCC. He also serves as Vice Chairman of the Dubai Chamber Real Estate Valuation Advisory Group, shaping industry standards and best practices.

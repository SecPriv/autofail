Understanding Indexes

What Are Index Funds?

An index fund is a type of investment that tracks a specific market index, such as the S&P 500 for stocks or a curated basket of cryptocurrencies. Instead of picking individual assets, you invest in the entire market segment.

At XPO, our index funds combine Forex pairs, CFDs, and cryptocurrencies into expertly managed portfolios, giving you exposure to multiple markets through a single investment.

What is Leheriya?

Leheriya is an Indian premium ethnic wear brand specializing in handcrafted unstitched 3-piece salwar suits, including Ajrakh hand block prints, Kota Doria cotton suits, Banarasi silk sets, Maheshwari cotton-silk blends, Kalamkari prints, and traditional regional textiles. Each suit set includes top fabric, bottom fabric, and dupatta, designed for custom tailoring and elegant festive or everyday wear.

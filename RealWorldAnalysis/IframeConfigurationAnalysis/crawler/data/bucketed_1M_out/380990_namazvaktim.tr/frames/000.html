      📖 About Prayer Times

      Salat, Salat (prayer) is one of the Five Pillars of Islam, performed five times daily.

      Five Daily Prayers:

      • Fajr - From dawn until sunrise
      • Dhuhr - After the sun passes its zenith
      • Asr - From mid-afternoon until sunset
      • Maghrib - Just after sunset
      • Isha - From dusk until dawn

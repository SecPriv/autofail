FAQ

Do the kits work on any bike?

Most bikes with open‑slotted dropouts and quick‑release wheels will work. Check dropout width and make sure your frame has space for the battery.

What are dropouts and how do I measure them?

Dropouts are the slots where your wheel axle sits. Measure the inside width — usually 135 mm for most quick‑release bikes. Match this to the option shown on the product page.

What countries do you ship to?

We ship to Australia, USA, UK, Canada, UAE, Oman, China, Taiwan, Ireland, Germany, France, Spain, Croatia, Russia, Cyprus, Estonia, Latvia, Lithuania, Slovakia, Slovenia, Italy, Netherlands, Belgium, Finland, Austria, Portugal, Greece, Bulgaria, Czech Republic, Denmark, Hungary, Poland, Romania, Sweden.

HOW DOES WARRANTY WORK?

Warranty coverage applies only if the product has been professionally installed by a qualified e-bike technician or licensed auto electrician.
Proof of professional installation, such as a technician’s invoice or service receipt, will be required to make a warranty claim.
Damage caused by incorrect installation, wiring, or modification voids the warranty.

How fast will my e‑bike go?

Check the product page for your kit’s top speed. Actual speed can vary with rider weight, terrain, and battery choice.

What’s the difference between sine wave and square wave controllers?

Sine wave: smoother, quieter, better control. slightly higher top speed.

Square wave: more punch off the line, a bit louder, slightly less range due to high power output

Are the kits waterproof?

Motors and controllers can handle rain and wet riding conditions. Avoid fully submerging parts or pressure‑washing.

How long will delivery take?

Express: 2–4 weeks. Standard: 40–70 days. Production time is usually 5–10 business days.

Frequently Asked Questions

Free Trial

Can I try Dext before committing to a subscription?

Yes, we offer a free 14 day trial so you can experience Dext’s features and benefits firsthand. No obligation to buy, no credit card required.

What happens after the trial ends?

At the end of your trial, you can choose a subscription plan that suits your firm. If you don’t upgrade, your account will be paused and no charges will be made.

Do I need a credit card to start a trial?

No, you can start your free trial without entering any payment details.


Does Dext provide support What support is available to help my business get started?

Yes! We provide onboarding guidance, help center articles, and dedicated customer support. Whether you’re setting up your account or scaling usage across your business, our team is ready to help you succeed.

Product and subscription

What is Dext, and how can it help my business?

Dext is a bookkeeping automation software that helps businesses save time and reduce manual work by automating the collection, processing, and storage of receipts, invoices and business documents. You can also manage and approve employee expenses, connect to ecommerce stores and sync all of your data to your accounting software.

Do I need to be an accountant to use Dext?

Not at all. Dext is also suitable for business owners and their teams. Its user-friendly interface makes it easy to upload, track, and manage documents. Your accountant or bookkeeper can also connect to your account for seamless collaboration.

Can Dext integrate with the tools I already use?

Yes! Dext integrates with major accounting software like QuickBooks Online, Xero, Sage, Zoho and more. It also connects to popular e-commerce platforms like Shopify, Etsy and Ebay and more.

What kind of businesses use Dext?

Businesses of all kinds—from retail and hospitality to construction and professional services—use Dext to streamline their financial admin. Whether you manage dozens or thousands of transactions monthly, Dext scales with your needs.

Is Dext suitable for teams and multi-user environments?

Absolutely. Dext allows multiple team members to upload and manage documents. You can control access levels and monitor submissions.

How much does Dext cost?

You can customise your Dext plan to meet your needs depending on how many users and documents you have. View our pricing here.

Document Submission and Data Security

Can my firm upload documents on behalf of clients?

Yes. Accountants and bookkeepers can submit documents on behalf of clients using any of the supported upload methods.

Can my clients upload their own documents to Dext?

Absolutely. Clients can send documents by email, mobile app, auto-fetch from accounts like WhatsApp, or upload directly to their Dext account.

Is Dext GDPR compliant?

Yes. Dext is fully GDPR compliant. Your data is encrypted, securely stored in the cloud for 10 years, and access can be controlled by user permissions.

How secure is my financial data on Dext?

Your data security is our top priority. Dext uses encrypted storage and follows best practices for data security and compliance - meaning we are GDPR and ISO compliant for all documents. You can read more about Dext’s compliance here.

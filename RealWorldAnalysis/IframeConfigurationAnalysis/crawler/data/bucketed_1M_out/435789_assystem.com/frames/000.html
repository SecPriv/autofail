Nuclear power: a reliable and resilient response to the growth of data centres

For data centres, SMRs represent a potential game-changer: a secure, localised, low carbon power source capable of supporting both resilience and sustainability. It’s an emerging frontier where digital infrastructure and clean energy engineering intersect. Yet the path to nuclear-powered data centres isn’t purely technical, it’s shaped significantly by the regulatory landscape that governs both industries.

    Tatkaal Advisory

    1. Before applying under Tatkaal scheme, applicant needs to check eligibility criteria, “Categories not under Tatkaal Scheme”- Hindi / English
    2. Applicants applying under Tatkaal scheme need to fill the "Undertaking for Tatkaal Scheme"- Hindi / English. Applicants are required to sign the undertaking and bring it to the Passport Office for submission at the time of appointment.

    Alert on Fake Websites related to Passport Services

    It has come to the notice of the Ministry that many fraudulent websites and mobile applications are collecting data from applicants and also levying additional hefty charges for filling up the online application form and scheduling appointment for Passport and related services. Some of these fake website are registered in the domain name *.org, *.in, *.com. such as : www.indiapassport.org, www.online-passportindia.com, www.passportindiaportal.in, www.passport-india.in, www.passport-seva.in, www.applypassport.org

    It is therefore advised to all citizens applying for Indian Passport and related services that they should not visit to the above mentioned fraudulent websites or make any kind of payment. The official website of the Ministry of External Affairs, Government of India, for applying Passport services is www.passportindia.gov.in

    Alternatively, applicants may also use the Passport Seva mobile App which can be downloaded from Android and iOS App store.

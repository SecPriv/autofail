About Us

Aseema is a service organisation started way back in the year 1992 with the sole aim of helping the prospective brides and grooms to find their match at one place. We only collect data and assort it under the different subcastes of Brahmins. We have high success rate. People from far away places throughout the country and even abroad come here to register.

We are proud to say that we have been regularly sponsoring economically weaker sections for their education. Right now we have around 20-25 students studying under this project.

Your subscriptions not only takes some of the needs of the organisation and its staff but also is being put to good use.

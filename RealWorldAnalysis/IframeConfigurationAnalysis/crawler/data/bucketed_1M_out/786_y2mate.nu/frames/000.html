How to convert and download a YouTube video:

  1. Open YouTube (youtube.com) and search for a video you would like to download. Copy the link from your browser's address bar. Our converter accepts the following YouTube link formats:
    • youtube.com/watch?v=videoId
    • youtube.com/embed/videoId
    • youtube.com/shorts/videoId
    • youtu.be/videoId
    Please note: links to live videos are not supported. Paste the link into the converter above.
  2. Choose the download format. You can select between the following options:
    • MP3 (up to 240 minutes / 192 kbps)
    • MP4 (up to 30 minutes / 360p)
    If you do not choose a format, the video will be converted to an MP3 file by default. Click the “Convert” button.
  3. The conversion will start. Depending on the length of the video, this process may take a while. Once the conversion is complete, you can download the file by clicking the “Download” button.

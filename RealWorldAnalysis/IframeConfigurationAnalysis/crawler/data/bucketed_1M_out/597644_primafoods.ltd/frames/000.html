Job Benefits for Foreign Workers

Prima Foods Italy ensures that all employees, whether skilled or unskilled, receive a range of benefits, making their transition to working in the Italy smoother and more comfortable.

  • Visa Sponsorship: The company sponsors eligible foreign workers with Italy work visas.
  • Free Accommodation: Workers receive free housing or subsidized living arrangements near their workplace.

  • Yearly Bonus: Employees receive performance-based bonuses annually.

  • Meal Benefits: Some positions may offer free or discounted meals.

Eligibility Criteria

To apply for a job at Prima Foods Italy, candidates must meet the following eligibility requirements:

  • Age Limit: 20-50 years old.
  • Educational Background: Matric (High School) to Master’s Degree holders can apply.
  • Work Experience: Minimum 6 months of relevant experience preferred (not mandatory for some roles).
  • Language Requirement: Basic English communication skills are required.
  • Health Requirements: Candidates must pass a medical fitness test.

Required Documents for Job Application

Candidates applying for a visa-sponsored job at Prima Foods Italy need to provide the following documents:

  • Valid Passport (with at least 6 months of validity)
  • CNIC/NIC (National Identity Card)
  • Passport-Size Photographs (3-4 recent photos)
  • Updated CV/Resume (Work experience and skills must be mentioned)
  • Educational and Experience Certificates (if applicable)
  • Character Certificate (Issued by local authorities or previous employer)
  • Driver’s License (For driving-related jobs)

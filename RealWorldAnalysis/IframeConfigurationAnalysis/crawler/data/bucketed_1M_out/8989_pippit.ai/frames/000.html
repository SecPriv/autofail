Frequently Asked Questions

What is Pippit AI?

Pippit AI is your simplest creative agent, designed to streamline and enhance your content production process. By reducing costs and increasing efficiency, Pippit's AI video generator & image generator empowers you to quickly create impactful content in digital space.

Who is Pippit AI designed for?

Pippit AI Agent is designed for anyone looking to simplify and automate content creation, whether you’re a creator, marketer, business owner, educator, or AI enthusiast. From marketing to viral social videos, Pippit's AI video generator empowers you to create diverse content fast and easily.

How do I create my first video using Pippit Agent?

To generate a video with Pippit’s Video Agent, go to Pippit's homepage, choose a video model, upload any link, media, or file as a reference, and enter a detailed prompt describing what you want.You can customize the aspect ratio, language, and duration with our AI video generator. Once it's sent, simply wait for the result. Meanwhile, you can continue chatting with Pippit’s Video Agent to create the next video.

How do I create my first image using Pippit's Image Agent?

To create visuals with Pippit’s Image Agent, also go to Pippit's homepage. Choose "Create image" in the "Tools" entrance. Then simply describe the image you want in the prompt box.You can generate multiple images, blend or merge them, and customize the aspect ratio with Pippit's AI image generator. You can also switch or remove the background of your image at any time.

What makes Pippit different from other AI content generation agents？

Pippit is your simplest creative agent powered by industry-leading AI models. It understands your intent and transforms a single prompt into a fully realized creation.

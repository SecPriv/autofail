Epic Waters Indoor Waterpark

Experience an EPIC adventure any time of year at Epic Waters, the premier indoor waterpark in Grand Prairie, Texas. Ranked as a Top 3 Indoor Waterpark in the Nation by USA TODAY for three consecutive years (2023, 2024, and 2025), our 80,000-square-foot facility offers a world-class experience right in the heart of the Dallas-Fort Worth area.

Whether you’re looking for a relaxing escape or a heart-pounding thrill, our massive, climate-controlled facility under a retractable roof offers the ultimate year-round destination for families across the Dallas-Fort Worth area.

Rain or shine, the excitement never stops in our 80,000-square-foot park. Thrill-seekers can brave the Lasso Loop or experience the weightless rush of the Yellowjacket Drop. For those looking for fun things to do with kids, our epic splashpad and the longest indoor lazy river in North Texas provide hours of entertainment. You can even learn to surf on the FlowRider waves or hit the high scores in our massive on-site arcade.

If you are looking for the best indoor waterpark in North Texas or a perfect staycation near Dallas, Epic Waters is your EPIC weather-proof destination.

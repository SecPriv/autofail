      Innovative Learning Experiences
      Top-Tier Training Courses
      The content prepared by over 800 competent instructors from expert institutions in relevant fields is available to public sector employees to acquire the necessary knowledge, skills, and competencies.
      Webinar & Virtual Classroom
      High-participation webinars or interactive live classes can be conducted simultaneously in accordance with the needs of public institutions.
      Sign Language Interpreted Courses
      Over 50 sign language supported courses are provided to increase the accessibility of educational content for hearing-impaired public sector employees.
      Need-based Training Courses
      Tailored training content, derived from a Training Needs Analysis, is provided to address the personal and professional development needs of public servants.
      Various Learning Settings
      In addition to video content, various materials such as audio narration, e-books, SCORM compatible interactive content, webinar recordings, and presentations are available.
      Thematic Training Programs
      Customizable training programs tailored to national strategies such as human rights, zero waste, artificial intelligence, etc., or specific to institutions/unions are offered.
      Learning Analytics and Gamification
      The development process of a learning analytics dashboard is underway, which will enhance public servants' learning experience and allow them to track their progress in real-time.
      Content Recommender System
      A system for recommending educational content based on the individual learning preferences of public servants and the preferences of colleagues with similar titles/institutions is under development.
      Individualized Learning Plan
      A module is being developed where public servants can create and track individualized learning plans tailored to their own time and areas of interest.

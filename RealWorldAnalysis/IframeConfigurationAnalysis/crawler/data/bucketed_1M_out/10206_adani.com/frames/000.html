SECURITY ALERT - Beware of Fraudulent Communications

It has come to our attention that certain unauthorised individuals and entities are falsely claiming to represent the Adani Group and attempting to solicit money from members of the public under the pretext of vendor registration, recruitment, job offers, interviews, training programmes or other business opportunities.

The Adani Group and its entities do not charge any fees or request any payment for:

  • Vendor or supplier registration or onboarding
  • Job applications, recruitment processes or employment offers
  • Interviews, training programmes or onboarding formalities
  • Any request for money in connection with the above should be treated as fraudulent.

We strongly advise individuals and businesses to exercise caution and verify all communications exclusively through official Adani Group channels and domain-based email addresses.

The Adani Group and its entities disclaim any liability for loss, damage, financial harm, data compromise or claims arising from dealings with unauthorised persons or fraudulent communications.

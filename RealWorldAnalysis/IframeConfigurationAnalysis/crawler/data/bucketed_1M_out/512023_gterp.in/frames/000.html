Why Choose Us?

Overseas Education, Immigration and Training Consultants

Global Tree is a one-stop-shop where you can access end-to-end support for your international career plans. Starting with test/exam preparation for study or settle abroad and ending with visa procedures, Global Tree assists you with every step along the way till you arrive in your dream country. We are the “India’s most trusted consultant” having the best experts in the city with ample experience in study abroad, immigration, work, PR services etc.

Migration, higher education, or even investing in another nation is major life decisions that must be made with extreme caution. Even a minor error can result in a rejection. Our professionals have been in the industry for 20 years and have dealt with a wide range of situations, including last-minute blunders. As a result, having counseled over a million students and professionals, Global Tree is the finest choice for visa and study abroad counseling.
